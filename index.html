About Space

Space is a pioneer in space exploration, committed to making humanity a multiplanetary species. Founded in 2023, we push the boundaries of technology and innovation to reach the stars.

Our Mission

We aim to make space accessible for everyone and expand humanity to other planets through cutting-edge rocket technology and sustainable space travel.

Technologies

  • Reusable rocket boosters to reduce costs.
  • Advanced AI navigation systems for deep space missions.
  • Sustainable fuel technologies for cleaner launches.
  • Next-gen satellite deployment techniques.

Our Rockets

Falcon Light

A lightweight rocket designed for small satellite launches and quick deployment.

StarMax

Powerful, reusable, and engineered for deep space exploration and cargo missions.

Orbital Cruiser

Designed for crewed missions to space stations and lunar orbit.

Recent Launches

  • March 2025: StarMax successfully delivered cargo to Mars orbit.
  • January 2025: Falcon Light launched 20 satellites into low Earth orbit.
  • November 2024: Orbital Cruiser completed its first crewed test flight.
